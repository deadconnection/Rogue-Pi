How does the Sky WiFi Roaming Service work?

Welcome to the Sky WiFi Roaming Service.

To access the WiFi Service at this Hotspot and other Hotspots operated by Sky WiFi or our partners you will need to login or create an account.

Your account information will be collected by Sky WiFi (and where you roam on to hotspots operated by a partner, shared with that partner) to enable Sky WiFi (or our partner) to provide the WiFi service to you.

For more information about the Sky WiFi Roaming Service or To find other Hotspots near you please visit our Hotspot Finder.
